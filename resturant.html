Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Samosa

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Bhajia

    Sliced potatoes coated in seasoned gram flour batter and deep-fried until crispy. Often served with chutney or tomato sauce.

  • Mishkaki

    Skewers of marinated and grilled meat, often beef, chicken, or lamb, similar to kebabs. Served with a spicy sauce or chutney.

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Bruschetta

    Grilled bread topped with diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, and olive oil.

  • Pani Puri

    Hollow, crispy puris filled with a mixture of spicy tamarind water, chickpeas, potatoes, and tangy chutney. A burst of flavors and textures in every bite.

Main Courses

  • Nyama Choma

    rilled meat, typically goat or beef, seasoned with salt and sometimes spices. It’s often served with kachumbari (a tomato and onion salad) and ugali (a maize flour porridge).

  • Chicken Alfredo

    Fettuccine pasta with a creamy Alfredo sauce, topped with grilled chicken.

  • Grilled Ribeye Steak

    A juicy, tender ribeye steak grilled to perfection, served with a side of garlic mashed potatoes and steamed vegetables.

  • Beef Stroganoff

    Beef stroganoff, a classic Italian dish, made with tomatoes, basil, garlic, and olive oil, often served with a side of meatballs.

  • Lasagna

    Layered lasagna filled with meatballs, cheese, and tomato sauce, served with a side of marinated artichokes and grilled onions.

  • Shawarma

    Marinated meat (chicken, lamb, or beef) cooked on a vertical rotisserie, sliced thinly, and served in pita bread with fresh vegetables and sauces like garlic or tahini. It’s known for its flavorful, juicy texture and is a popular street food.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Tiramisu

    Classic Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream.

  • Cheesecake

    Rich and creamy cheesecake with a graham cracker crust and berry compote.

  • Chocolate Lava Cake

    A warm, molten chocolate cake with a gooey center that flows out when cut, typically served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

  • Banoffee Pie

    A dessert made from bananas, cream, and toffee on a biscuit base, sometimes topped with chocolate shavings or grated chocolate.
